Cleaner audio. On your iPhone.

CleanCut is an iPhone audio editor with three tools — Smart Cut, Denoise, and Merge. Every step runs on-device. Your audio never leaves your phone.

Smart Cut

Detects filler words ("um," "uh," "like"), long silences, and dead air. Review every cut on a waveform timeline before you export. Auto-transcript with .txt / .srt / .vtt export.

Denoise

DeepFilterNet3 noise reduction, on-device. An intensity slider plus an A/B toggle so you can hear exactly what changed before committing.

Merge

Drag clips onto a timeline, set crossfades, export as M4A or WAV. Loudness auto-normalized to BS.1770 broadcast standard.

On-device. Always.

CleanCut runs entirely on your iPhone. No upload. No server. No analytics SDK. No third-party tracker. The transcription model, the noise-reduction model, and every edit you make stay on the device. Turn on airplane mode — CleanCut still works.

Free to start: 3 Smart Cut + 3 Denoise sessions a day, files up to 5 min (Smart Cut) or 3 min (Denoise), unlimited Merge with up to 3 clips. CleanCut Pro removes every limit.

Built for iPhone

  • iOS Share Extension — clean a voice memo from Voice Memos or Mail without leaving the app you're in.
  • Files app and iCloud Drive support for import and export.
  • Light and dark mode, both first-class.
  • Background processing with system push notifications when long files finish (Pro).
